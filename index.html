Our Brands

MyCourse.lk

Mycourse.lk is our consumer brand that offers digital service to find higher educational pathways for students in Sri Lanka.

  • 01 Why we create mycourse.lk?

    Every year, millions of students finish their student life and seek new opportunities for their higher education in Sri Lanka. So, finding the path you want is a bit challenging due to the different types of educational institutions. So as a solution, we created a platform with institutes across the country to find the best option for students in Sri Lanka.

  • 02 What are the benefits?
    • Students can design their own educational path from basic certification to PhD level with just one click.
    • Up to date contents.
    • Daily updates of events and scholarships.
    • Detail descriptions of courses.
    • Ability to easily compare courses.
